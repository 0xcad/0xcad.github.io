Car Seat Headrest Lore

98-274: You already know that Car Seat Headrest is an American indie rock band in Seattle, WA. But what do you know about the lore behind the band and its founding member Will Toledo? In CSH Lore, students listen to and explore the works of CSH and its associated acts, unravelling the mysteries behind some of indie's most defining songs and albums.

How to Register

Car Seat Headrest Lore is a student taught course. Students intending to take this course must register for 98-274 on Student Information Online (SIO). Students who receive a Pass/Satisfactory final grade receive three (3) Free Elective Units. CMU students may use no more than nine (9) Free Elective Units towards degree requirements, per University policy.

Analysed Works

In this class we will analyse Car Seat Headrest the discography starting with 1 (2010) up to Making a Door Less Open (2020). Singles, covers, interviews, and other CSH media will also be consumed. This also includes works from the side-project bands 1 Trait Danger and Skinny Teeth. Students can find resources for this course in the CMU library course reserves.
