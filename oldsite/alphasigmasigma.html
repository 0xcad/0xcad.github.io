What is AΣΣ

Alpha Sigma Sigma is CMU's most exclusive fraternity living community. We value our rigor, prestige, and leadership ability. Past brothers include Joshua Jones Rutherford-Washington III, President Bill Clinton, and Andrew Carnegie.

Joining AΣΣ

Due to the nature of our prestige, our selection process is extremely selective. Applications for students are closed by default; permission to apply is expressly given on the basis of exceptional merit and performance. A team of former alum is tasked with defining these standards.

Contact

Students are asked not to contact the fraternity. All of our operations are conducted in-house. Students who believe they must contact Alpha Sigma Sigma are not the type that the fraternity accepts.
